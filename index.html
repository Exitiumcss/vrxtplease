    What “Admin” usually is

    Tap or click to flip →

    A role with dangerous permissions: kick/ban, manage channels, manage roles, webhooks, server settings. Owners rarely hand this to strangers.

    What owners want instead

    Someone who doesn’t need full admin — just the minimum permissions to do the job (mod, events, bots, etc.).

    Two paths that work

    1. Join, behave, be visible for weeks — not hours.
    2. Help without being asked: welcome people, answer FAQs, report issues calmly.
    3. Ask for mod first (timeout, delete messages) — not Administrator.
    4. After consistency, owner may duplicate mod role and add one dangerous perm at a time.
    1. Define exactly what ASAP needs: “manage this category only” or “manage bot role below X.”
    2. Owner creates a new role with only those toggles — never the Administrator checkbox unless the whole server is yours.
    3. Put the role below the owner’s top role so hierarchy stays sane.
    4. Document it in a pinned message so future mods understand why that role exists.
